Why We Built This

Built by people who lived in the denial queue.

Most healthcare AI tools are built by software companies that mapped billing workflows from the outside. They're technically sophisticated but operationally naive. They automate the easy parts and leave the hard parts to your staff.

Clientele AI was built differently. Our team spent years inside the denial queue, working prior authorizations, posting payments, chasing underpaid claims, and navigating the modifier decisions that the wrong code selection makes catastrophically expensive. We built automation for the work we knew — because we had done it ourselves.

The result is a platform where automation handles volume, speed, and pattern recognition — and where certified RCM professionals handle complexity, exceptions, and anything that requires clinical context. We call this Human-Guided Automation, and it is the only model that produces consistent results in specialty healthcare billing.

Key Automations
  • Real-time eligibility verification across all major payers
  • Automated discrepancy detection and alert routing
  • Benefits breakdown including co-pay, deductible & coverage limits
  • Pre-visit eligibility summary pushed to provider & front-desk dashboards
  • Eligibility failure flagging before the patient arrives — not after the claim bounces
Why it matters

Eligibility errors are the #1 front-end cause of claim denials. By catching coverage issues before the visit, practices eliminate an entire category of avoidable rejection.

Security & Compliance

Architected for enterprise healthcare.

HIPAA-Compliant Architecture
All PHI handled per HIPAA Privacy & Security Rules. TLS 1.2+ in transit, AES-256 at rest. BAA executed with every client.
Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
Staff access scoped to role. Coders see coding workflows. Billers see billing queues. Providers see patient-facing dashboards. No cross-role data exposure.
Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
Required on all platform access points — provider app login, biller portal access, and administrative interfaces.
SOC2 Type II — In Progress
Full SOC2 Type II audit underway. Expected completion: Q3 2026. Security controls currently in place and audit-ready.
Audit Logging
Every action on PHI is logged, timestamped, and retrievable. Full audit trail for compliance reviews and incident response.
